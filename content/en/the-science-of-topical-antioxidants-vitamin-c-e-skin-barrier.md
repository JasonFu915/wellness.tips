## The Science: What Are Antioxidants and Why Do They Matter?

### Vitamin C: The Brightening Powerhouse

Vitamin C (or ascorbic acid) is a water-soluble antioxidant that works on the surface of your skin. It helps brighten your complexion, fade dark spots, and stimulate collagen production.

### Vitamin E: The Skin Barrier Guardian

Vitamin E is a fat-soluble antioxidant that works deeper in the skin. It protects cell membranes from damage and supports the skin's natural barrier function.
